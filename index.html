Why the MATH Tutoring at USC can be problematic

The Math tutoring here is problematic to me because often times there are far more students than the tutors can help.

Even when there are 4-5 tutors there is usually 30-40 students who need help. If we hired more tutors, we could reduce stress for the existing tutors and help the students more quickly and effectively.
